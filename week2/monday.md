## Past tense

## Aspects

- verbs can be perfective and imperfective
- infinitives are imperfective
- add a prefix to get perfective
- prefixes have their own meaning
- perfective is more common

### Prefixes

#### по- 
- has no meaning
- used only for aspect creation

#### вы-
- means "out"

#### до-
- means "finish"

#### пере-
- means "over/again"

#### про-
-means "through"
